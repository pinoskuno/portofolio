E-Pendaftaran

Developed a web-based system for Puskesmas Simpur Bandar Lampung to streamline patient registration and doctor schedule management. Utilized HTML, CSS, JavaScript, and Firebase Cloud Firestore for efficient data management, including create, update, and delete operations for patient and doctor data. Implemented check-in functionality and responsive design, ensuring compatibility across various device screen sizes. Enhanced operational efficiency by automating registration processes and improving data accessibility for healthcare staff.
