Harvestia

Bangkit academy Harvestia (Kotlin) Application Designer and Backend Developer Developed "Harvestia," a mobile application aimed at enhancing agricultural knowledge and disease management for rice crops. The app provides users with valuable content such as articles and news updates on rice farming, and features a main functionality of diagnosing rice plant diseases using computer vision technology. Contributed to the app by: Designing the user interface and ensuring a user-friendly experience.Developing the backend environment for managing and updating content related to articles and news.Adapting a web-based computer vision model for disease detection into a mobile-compatible TensorFlow Lite model, improving accessibility and performance.
